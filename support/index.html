Common Topics

A few quick answers to the questions people ask most often.

I have a billing or subscription question.

Purchases and subscriptions are managed through Apple’s App Store. If you need help understanding what happened or what to try next, contact support and we can point you in the right direction.

How do I restore purchases?

The app includes a Restore Purchases option, try using that first. If your access still does not return as expected, contact support.

The app did not generate suggestions as expected.

Please try again after checking your connection and input. If the issue keeps happening, email support with a short note about what you were trying to do.

I found a bug.

Send support a short description of the issue, your device model, iOS version, and what happened right before the problem appeared.
